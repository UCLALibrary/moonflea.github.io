Dear ALHHS Colleague:

The 2002 meeting of the Archivists and Librarians in the History of the Health Sciences is being held in Kansas City, Kansas, April 24 and 25, 2002. The conference hotel is the Westin Crown Center, 1 Pershing Road, Kansas City, Missouri.

Hotel and Room Reservations: Be sure to ask for the AAHM Room Block. Hotel reservations should be made directly with the hotel at 1-888-627-8538, or access information about the hotel at http://www.westin.com [ see attached hotel information ]. Please note that the deadline for reservations at the conference rate is March 26, 2002! The rates are:

$144 single
$154 double
$164 triple
$174 quad
Plus 12.1% room tax

Travel information with a map of the area is enclosed. The average high temperature in April in Kansas City is 65°F, with an average low temperature of 43°F. It is generally very pleasant, but cool.

This year the dinner will be sponsored by the Clendening Library for the History of Medicine at the University of Kansas, and will be held the evening before the program and tours. Please see enclosed registration form and agenda for additional information. Deadline for return of these forms is April 8, 2002.

The staff of the University of Kansas Medical Center Archives and the Clendening Library for the History of Medicine are looking forward to having you in Kansas City. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me by e-mail (nhulston@kumc.edu) or by phone (913-588-7243) or fax (913-588-7060).
