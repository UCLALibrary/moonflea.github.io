The Westin Crown Center is pleased to be hosting The American Association of the History of Medicine Convention in April of 2002. To ensure everything runs smoothly, we would like to take this opportunity to make the reservation process as easy as possible.
  1. Reservations need to be made no later than March 26, 2002.

  2. Reservations may be made by calling our toll free line 1-888-627-8538.

  3. Attendees must ask for The American Association of the History of Medicine

  4. Please have your credit card ready to guarantee a reservation. (You must put the reservation on your credit card first but can always send in a deposit check for the first night's estimated charge. Once the check has been received the reservation will be removed from the credit card.)

  5. Rates are $144 single, $154 double, $164 triple, and $174 quad (plus tax, currently 12.1%)

  6. Room types are on a request basis.

  7. A reservation can be canceled up to 24 hours before first night stay without penalty (if you fail to cancel the reservation within this time period, you will be charged for the first nights room/tax and surcharge).
Thank you again for choosing the Westin Crown Center. We look forward to your event!
