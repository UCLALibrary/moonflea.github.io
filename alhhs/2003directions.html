Directions to The Countway Library of Medicine from The Westin Hotel
(10 Huntington Avenue)

 

 The Countway Library is located on the Harvard Medical School campus, in the Longwood Medical Area, at 10 Shattuck St. Boston, MA 02115 (see the enclosed map for details.) We will be meeting in the Minot Room, on the fifth floor of the Library.

The Library is visible from Huntington Avenue between Longwood Ave. and Francis St. The Countway entrance is at the end of Shattuck Street, which runs parallel to Francis Street at the rear of the Brigham & Women's Hospital.

Nearest MBTA "T" Stop: Brigham Circle on the Green Line "E" Train:
If you are coming to the Countway from the Westin Copley, board the outbound "E" Line [Heath Street] MBTA Green Line train at Copley and ride past the Museum of Fine Arts to Brigham Circle
(approximately 10 minutes from Copley.) The Brigham Circle stop is directly behind the Countway Library.

Nearest MBTA Bus Stop: Harvard School of Public
Health, Bus #39, Huntington Ave.

Parking:
Street parking on Huntington Avenue @, $0.25 per 15 minutes, 2 hour maximum Brigham and Women's parking garage on Francis Street Children's Hospital parking garage on Longwood Avenue
