The ALHHS meets annually in conjunction with the American Association for the History of Medicine. The meetings usually consist of half-day programs on topics of interest to archivists, librarians and booksellers. Relevant local tours of historical interest are offered as part of the program. A business meeting is also conducted. A dinner is traditionally held the night before the meeting.

The Meeting is usually hosted by an ALHHS member and an associated institution. Joint meetings with other societies such as the Medical Museums Association have also been held.
