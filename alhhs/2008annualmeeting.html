2008 ALHHS Annual Meeting: Rochester, New York, April 9-10 (AAHM: April 10-13)
Hello, Everyone:
The registration materials for the April meeting in Rochester, N.Y. are going out in today's (February 11) mail. Except for those of you in Canada and the UK, you should have your packets by the end of this week.
If you have not received your packets by February 22 (a week from this Friday), please let me know and I'll get another set off to you.
I hope you will be able to attend this year's meeting. The banquet is always fun; the program committee has put together an imaginative program; and we'll tour the International Museum of Photography at the George Eastman House. It's eight degrees in Rochester at the moment, but it should be comparatively balmy by mid-April (mid-30s to high-40s).
Chris
