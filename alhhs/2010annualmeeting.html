2010 ALHHS Annual Meeting: Rochester, Minnesota, April 28-29  (AAHM: April 29 - May 2)
Dear Archivists and Librarians,

As you are making plans for 2010, please mark your calendar for April 28 and 29 to attend the 37th annual meeting of the Archivists and Librarians in the History of the Health Sciences, to be held at the Kahler Grand Hotel in Rochester, Minnesota. To learn more about the unique history of the hotel, which opened in 1921 and included operating suites, 150-bed convalescent unit and 220-room hotel, visit "About the Kahler".

The conference rate at the Kahler per night is $110 plus taxes (this is for single, double, triple or quadruple occupancy).

The Wednesday evening banquet will be $38 per person. The registration fee for the meeting on Thursday is $25 per person. Please print the registration form , complete it, and return it with payment to Arlene Shaner.

Look for all additional information in the current edition of the Watermark.

If you have any questions regarding the upcoming meeting, please do not hesitate to contact me.

I look forward to seeing you in April!

Renee Ziemer
