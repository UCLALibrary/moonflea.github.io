Registration Information

Please complete a separate Registration Form for each attendee.

 

Important notices from the Local Arrangements Committee
  • Accommodations/Hotel Reservations [Specific Instructions]: To make your hotel reservations, see "Meeting Hotel" section of the AAHM2014 website.
  • Dinner on on May 7: Osteria Via Stato has provided a menu for The Italian Dinner.

  • Lunch on May 8: Please see the caterer's menus, with detailed descriptions of Salads and Sandwichs and Wraps, before making your selection on page 3 of the Registration Form.

 

Step 1: Complete and return the entire Registration Form

  • Open the Registration Form. Print the blank form, complete it by hand, and snailmail it to Barbara Niss (ALHHS Treasurer; see address, below); OR
  • Open the Registration Form. Complete the “fillable PDF” Registration Form online, print it (one copy for you, one to mail), and snailmail it to Barbara Niss; OR
  • Open the Registration Form. Complete the “fillable PDF” Registration Form online; print a copy for yourself; save the completed form as a PDF file (use “Save a copy” icon in upper lefthand corner of the form); email the saved file to Barbara Niss at: alhhs.treasurer AT gmail.com

 

Step 2: Calculate the total payment you will make

  • $80.00  =  I will attend the Meeting ($30.00); I will attend the Dinner ($50.00)
  • $50.00  =  I will NOT attend the Meeting (30.00); I will attend the Dinner ($50.00)
  • $30.00  =  I will attend the Meeting ($30.00); I will NOT attend the Dinner ($0.00)

 

Step 3: Send your payment using payment option "ONLINE" or "CHECK"

  • ONLINE option:
    Select the correct "Total Payment Due for ALHHS Annual Meeting" option in the pull-down menu, below; click Pay Now button and complete your online payment PayPal:
    Total Payment Due for 2014 ALHHS Annual Meeting

     

  • CHECK option:
    1. Please make your check payable in U.S. Dollars to "ALHHS"
    2. Enclose your check with a printed copy of your registration Form
    3. Snailmail the envelope to Barbara Niss, ALHHS Treasurer (see address, below)
