29 - 30 April 2015

 

Registration Information

Please complete a separate Conference Registration Form for each attendee.

 

Important notices
  • Accommodations/Hotel Reservations [Specific Instructions]: To make your hotel reservations at the Omni New Haven Hotel, see "Meeting Hotel" section of the AAHM "New Haven 2015" website.
  • Dinner on on April 29: There will be a dinner buffet with choice of three entrees; details are on the conference registration form.
  • Lunch on April 30: Please see the conference registration form and select from 9 sandwich or salad choices.

 

Step 1: Complete and submit the entire Conference Registration Form (this is powered by Google Forms)

  • Open and complete the ALHHS/MeMA 2015 Conference Registration Form.
  • Before you "Submit" the form, print a copy for your own records.
  • If you will be paying by Check, print a second copy of the form to send with your snailmailed payment.
  • "Submit" the completed form.

 

Step 2: Calculate the total payment you will make

  • $80.00  =  I will attend the Meeting ($30.00); I will attend the Dinner ($50.00)
  • $50.00  =  I will NOT attend the Meeting (30.00); I will attend the Dinner ($50.00)
  • $30.00  =  I will attend the Meeting ($30.00); I will NOT attend the Dinner ($0.00)

 

Step 3: Send your payment using payment option "ONLINE" or "CHECK"

  • ONLINE option:
    Select the correct "2015 ALHHS/MeMA Conference Registration" option in the pull-down menu, below; click Pay Now button and complete your online payment with PayPal:
    2015 ALHHS/MeMA Conference Registration

     

  • CHECK option:
    1. Please make your check payable in U.S. Dollars to "ALHHS"
    2. Enclose your check with a printed copy of your Conference Registration Form
    3. Snailmail the envelope to Barbara Niss, ALHHS Treasurer (see address, below)
