Membership is open to librarians and archivists with responsibilities for collections and services in the history of the health sciences, and to any other persons interested in health science history collections.

 

Dues: $15.00 per year (U.S. Dollars)

 

Information may be obtained from:

Barbara Niss (ALHHS Treasurer)
14 Elmwood Ave.
Rye, New York 10580  USA

barbara.niss AT mssm.edu or alhhs.treasurer AT gmail.com

 

New Member application / Continuing Member renewal OPTION 1 (pay online):

  • Complete the Membership (New or Renewal) form, which is a "fillable PDF" file; save it as a PDF file; then email the file to ALHHS Treasurer Barbara Niss: alhhs.treasurer AT gmail.com

  • Select your Membership Category below, click Pay Now button, and complete online payment of US $15.00 through PayPal:
    ALHHS Membership Category

 

New Member application / Continuing Member renewal OPTION 2 (pay by check):

  • Complete the Membership (New or Renewal) form, which is a "fillable PDF" file; print a hardcopy

  • Mail the printed form, along with a check for US$15.00 payable to ALHHS, to:

    Barbara Niss (ALHHS Treasurer)
    14 Elmwood Ave.
    Rye, New York 10580  USA
