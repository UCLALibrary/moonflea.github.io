Photograph of Edgar Bowers with his Army Specialized Training Program roommates at 225 Joline Hall, Princeton University, from October to December, 1943.

Pictured from left to right are George F. Cronkite, Edgar Bowers, Sam Pillsbury, Hank Dows, and Frank Mullaly. Another roommate, William Heyl, who is the subject of Bowers?s poems "Elegy: for W.C.S.H." and "Late Winter Night," is not pictured.
