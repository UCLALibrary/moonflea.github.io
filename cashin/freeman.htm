Judith Freeman,
novelist, essayist, and critic, is the author of a collection of short stories and four novels, including The Chinchilla Farm, and most recently, Red Water, which was named one of the 100 Best Books of 2002 by the She is the recipient of a Guggenheim Fellowship in Fiction, the Western Heritage Award, and the Utah Book Award. Her stories and essays have appeared in a number of anthologies, and her work has been adapted to film. She lives in Los Angeles with her husband, artist-photographer Anthony Hernandez.
