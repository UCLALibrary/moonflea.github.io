Barbara Hodgson

Barbara Hodgson is the author, artist, and designer of non-fiction works as well as illustrated novels that are brought to life by an amazing array of archival photographs, antique postcards, and rare engravings. Among her acclaimed illustrated novels are Hippolyte's Island, The Sensualist, The Tattooed Map, and, most recently, The Lives of Shadows. Her non-fiction compliations include The Rat: A Perverse Miscellany, Opium: A Portrait of the Heavenly Demon, and No Place for a Lady: Tales of Adventurous Women Travellers. In addition to ther work as an author and designer, Hodgson is the cofounder of Byzantium Books, a book packaging company dedicated to challenging the form of the book. She is an inveterate traveler and collector with a chronic case of wanderlust.
