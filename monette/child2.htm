Letter to Paul Monette from Avon editorial director Robert B. Wyatt. He gives details of the design and marketing of The Gold Diggers. It is not stated that this is as a "gay novel," but it seems slanted to the emerging gay market. The design is stylish Art Deco and the cover illustration is by Stavrinos. He was also doing an illustration for an excerpt of the novel being published in the gay lifestyle and erotica journal Blueboy. 1978 September 15
