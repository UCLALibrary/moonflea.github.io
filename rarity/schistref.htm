University Librarian Lawrence Clark Powell founded the Department in 1946 and appointed its first head in 1947.

1947 - 1950
Neal Harlow

Harlow is shown as Los Angeles newspaperman and writer Matt Weinstock gives the manuscript of his book Muscatel at Noon. They seem to be in the first quarters of the Department in what is now the Powell Library Building. Early 1950s

1950 - 1951
Andrew H. Horn

Horn, who would succeed Powell as dean of the Library School, is shown center, with staff member J. Gregg Layne, left, and staff member Ralph Lyon Jr., right. In the exhibits area, Powell Library Building quarters. ca. 1951.

1951 - 1971
Wilbur Jordan Smith

Smith is shown at the podium possibly in Special Collections. Seated is W.W. Robinson, first president, Friends of the UCLA Library. Early 1950s.

Victoria Steele

The current head of Special Collections is not only a UCLA alumna, but also knows the UCLA library well, having previously held several positions in the library. An art historian, she is a former Fulbright Fellow to the United Kingdom.

The curators of rare books, manuscripts, maps, and photographs have had various titles and sometimes various combined responsibilities.

  • Mary De Wolfe, 1948 - 1952, curator of collections
  • Andrew H. Horn, 1948 - 1950, manuscripts
  • H. Richard Archer, 1952 - 1953, books
  • Ralph Lyon Jr., 1951 - 1952 and 1953 - 1955, manuscripts, newspapers (and archives)
  • F. Brooke Whiting, 1957 - 1983, books and literary manuscripts (and assistant head)
  • Ralph Johnson, 1958 - 1964, rare maps, prints, and graphic arts
  • Evert Volkerz, 1964 - 1968?, rare maps, prints, and graphic arts
  • Saundra B. Taylor, 1968 - 1974, historical manuscripts (and archives)
  • Hilda Bohem, 1969 - 1986, maps, prints, and graphic arts, then also photographs
  • James G. Davis, 1983 - 1997, literary manuscripts and books, then books

  • Anne Caiger, 1975 - present, historical manuscripts (and archives), then all manuscripts, maps, and graphic arts and photographs
  • Charlotte B. Brown, faculty papers (and Assistant Head and University Archivist), 1991 - 2001
    University Archivist, 2001 - present
  • Daniel J. Slive, 1998 - present, books
