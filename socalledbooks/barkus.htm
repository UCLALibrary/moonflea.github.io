Mariona Barkus

Mariona Barkus has been creating and exhibiting artists' books for over 20 years and is recognized as one of the outstanding contributors to this art form. Her work is in many collections including Yale University, UCLA, Orange County Museum of Art, and The Museum of Modern Art Library. She has received grants for her works from the City of Los Angeles, Women's Studio Workshop, and the New York State Council on the Arts.
