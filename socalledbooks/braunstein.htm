Terry Braunstein

Terry Braunstein has just returned from a second residency at Yaddo, an artists' community located in Saratoga Springs, New York. She has been creating artists' books since 1972 and has produced more than 70 unique books and others in limited editions. She has also brought her work to a broader audience in the form of public art, including a permanent art piece for a Los Angeles Metro station and an installation commissioned by the Los Angeles County Museum of Art. She has received multiple awards and commissions from all over the world.

On Exhibit

Old
Long Beach, 1992
Sculpted altered book, photomontage with mixed media
Unique

"Like visual archaeology, my work always begins by exploring the world through research and scientific observation. I collect various kinds of ephemera and bring them together in collage. Old magazines, encyclopedias, books, and maps have come to form the archive material used in my photomontage-based work. The resulting images address issues of daily life and personal memory. "
