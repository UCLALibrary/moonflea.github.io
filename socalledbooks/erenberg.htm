Sam Erenberg

Sam Erenberg has earned a world-wide reputation as a painter, book artist, and installation artist. His works have been exhibited at major museums and public spaces, and he has had solo exhibitions at the Santa Barbara Museum of Art; the Skirball Cultural Center, Los Angeles; and the Gallery of Fine Arts at Ohio State University.

On Exhibit

The Playboy Reader
Santa Monica, 1995
Fifty-two drawings: gouache on perforated Braille paper, cloth-covered box
Unique

"Modernist art is still the work I prefer, especially European modernist art. It is the operations of modernism, the mechanisms of modernism that seem to have failed our culture. Not that post-modernism is any less or more critical than the modernist dictum -- it is just that art has to reinvent itself in every generation ..."
